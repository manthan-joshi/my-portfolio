§ 02 / Work

Things I've built and broken.

A selection of projects spanning graduate research, undergrad thesis work, coursework, and the business side of biotech. Some are wet lab, some are decks, some are both.

HPRT1 Graduate Project · 2025

CRISPR/Cas9 knock-in and knock-out in CHO cells.

The full CRISPR workflow at the HPRT1 locus. Cas9 RNP complex prep, CRISPRmax transfection, Sanger sequencing, and ICE analysis for INDEL detection.

CurcuNano Biotech Enterprise · 2025

A curcumin LNP pitch for MRSA.

Pipeline and Growth Story workstream for a team pitch building a curcumin lipid nanoparticle platform targeting refractory MRSA, an antibiotic-resistant infection with real unmet clinical need.

Case Study↗
Atlas Regulatory · 2025

CGT oversight under MHRA and Health Canada.

Graduate regulatory module on cell and gene therapy approval pathways in the UK and Canada, with case studies on Kymriah (NICE and MHRA coordination) and Abecma (NOC with Conditions).

Case Study↗
M4 Undergrad Thesis · 2023

Solubilization and purification of a recombinant membrane protein.

Optimized recombinant expression in E. coli BL21(DE3), Ni-NTA affinity chromatography, and a novel Triton X-100 and Tween 20 solubilization protocol. Awarded first rank in the 2019 batch.

Case Study↗
Butterfly Cell and Gene Therapies · 2024

Gene therapy for Epidermolysis Bullosa.

Class project on the genetic condition where skin is as fragile as butterfly wings. Worked through CRISPR, MSCs, and Vyjuvek, the first FDA-approved topical gene therapy for any disease.

Case Study↗
Alveolus Stem Cells · 2024

Lung repair via gene-edited MSCs for COPD.

Grant proposal for the Stem Cells and Regeneration course. Reversing lung damage using SP-C gene-edited mesenchymal stem cells in a mouse model, with CRISPR and AAV vectors.

Case Study↗
Satellite Gene Therapy · 2024

Muscle regeneration via AAV9 and IGF-1.

Gene therapy proposal exploring how AAV9-delivered IGF-1 can support skeletal muscle regeneration while minimizing fibrosis. Targeting growth factor expression post-injury to activate satellite cells.

Case Study↗
Taxonomer Bioinformatics · 2025

A taxon search engine, FastAPI and Dash.

Web app for searching taxonomic data with filters like "starts with," "contains," or "ends with." Built with FastAPI, SQLModel, and Dash. A bench guy writing backend, with backup.
